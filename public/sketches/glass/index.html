Voronoi → Lead Came → Light Pool

Drop in any image and it gets re-cut: K-means posterizes the source into a small palette, connected components find color regions, Voronoi sub-cells subdivide flat areas, and Douglas-Peucker simplifies the outlines into cuttable polygons.

The glass texture is projected through the plane along the sun direction to pool colored light on the floor; the volume between is raymarched for god rays. Drop an image · drag to orbit · scroll to zoom.
